Hi, I am Stephanie Major.

I am a native French speaker who immigrated to the United States in 1998. I am an American citizen. I did a college degree in public accounting in Canada and my first job after college was in a CPA firm. After a few years, I moved on to corporate finance in the banking sector. When we immigrated to United States, I had an opportunity in the telecom sector in analysis and I took it. Then I had kids, two wonderful boys, and I stayed at home. I have been back on the job market since June 2014. Looking into the market and liking new challenges, I decided to become a software developer. But, I quickly realized that it wasn't for me. I need more action, more social interactions. Prompted my by software mini experience, I decided to learn more about Product and Project Management. So, here I am learning and getting new skills that are inline with my personality and business experience.
